# Privacy Policy

**ThinkDeck — AI-Powered Decision Intelligence**

*Last Updated: March 2, 2026*

---

## 1. Introduction

ThinkDeck ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the ThinkDeck mobile application (the "App").

By using ThinkDeck, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Decision Content**: Questions, responses, and analysis data you input during thinking model sessions (SWOT, 5 Whys, Decision Matrix, First Principles, Six Thinking Hats, Gap Analysis, Pyramid Principle, Regret Minimization) and AI Strategic Agent conversations.
- **Strategy Reports**: Generated reports including goals, action items, risk assessments, and confidence scores.
- **Account Data**: Language preferences, subscription status, and app settings.

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Analytics**: Session frequency, models used, completion rates, and feature engagement (collected anonymously).
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and debugging purposes.
- **Crash Reports**: Anonymous crash logs to improve app stability.

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.
- We do **not** track your location.
- We do **not** sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide Core Functionality**: Process your decision analysis through thinking models and the AI Strategic Agent.
- **Generate Reports**: Create strategy reports based on your conversation and analysis data.
- **Improve the App**: Analyze anonymous usage patterns to enhance features and user experience.
- **Manage Subscriptions**: Process and manage your subscription through Apple's in-app purchase system.
- **Send Notifications**: Deliver daily motivational reminders (with your permission) to support your decision-making practice.

---

## 4. AI Processing & Third-Party Services

### 4.1 Google Gemini API

ThinkDeck uses Google's Gemini API to power:
- The AI Strategic Agent (multi-turn conversational analysis)
- Strategy report generation
- AI-powered structural validation and coaching

Your conversation content is sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. Google's usage of this data is governed by [Google's API Terms of Service](https://developers.google.com/terms) and [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

**Important**: We do not store conversation data on external servers beyond what is necessary for real-time AI processing. Your session data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via Supabase for cross-device access.

### 4.2 Supabase

We use Supabase for optional cloud data storage and synchronization. Data stored includes session history and generated reports. Supabase's privacy practices are governed by their [Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

### 4.3 Apple Services

- **StoreKit**: For subscription management and in-app purchases.
- **Push Notifications (APNs)**: For daily engagement reminders, if you opt in.

---

## 5. Data Storage & Security

- **Local Storage**: Your decision sessions, analysis data, and generated reports are stored locally on your device using Swift's native data persistence.
- **Encryption**: All data transmitted between the App and third-party services uses TLS/SSL encryption.
- **No External Accounts**: ThinkDeck does not require you to create an account with us. Your data belongs to you.

---

## 6. Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app data.
- **Cloud Data**: If synced, retained until you request deletion.
- **AI Processing**: Conversation data sent to the Gemini API is processed in real-time and not retained by us beyond the session.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: View all data stored in the app at any time.
- **Delete**: Remove any session, report, or all app data by deleting sessions from the Strategy Vault or uninstalling the app.
- **Opt Out**: Disable notifications and analytics at any time through the app's Settings.
- **Data Portability**: Share or export your strategy reports via the app's share functionality.

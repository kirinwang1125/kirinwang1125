## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **BOOMO** ("we," "our," or "us"). BOOMO is an AI-powered romance social simulation platform that creates immersive, personalized experiences through conversational AI characters.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our iOS application and related services (collectively, the "Services").

### 2.3 AI Processing Data

To provide realistic AI character interactions, we process:

- **Conversation Context:** Recent chat history (sliding window of last 20 turns)
- **Character Preferences:** Your preferred character types and interaction patterns
- **Memory Extraction:** Key facts about your preferences extracted from conversations
- **Narrative State:** Story progression data and relationship stages

**Important:** While we process conversation data to improve AI responses, we implement strict data minimization practices:
- Raw conversation data is retained for **90 days maximum** for service continuity
- Extracted memories are stored as anonymized, structured data
- AI training does not use your personal conversations

## 4. Data Storage & Security

### 4.1 Storage Location & Providers

Your data is stored securely using:

| Provider | Service | Data Stored | Location |
|----------|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Supabase** | PostgreSQL Database | User profiles, chat history, memories | United States (AWS US-East-1) |
| **Apple** | iCloud/Screen Time | App preferences, iCloud backup | User's iCloud region |

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

- 🔐 **End-to-End Encryption:** Sensitive data encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
- 🗄️ **Database Encryption:** At-rest encryption using AES-256
- 🚫 **Access Controls:** Strict role-based access for internal systems
- 📝 **Audit Logging:** All data access is logged and monitored
- 🧪 **Security Testing:** Regular penetration testing and vulnerability assessments

### 4.3 Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period | Deletion Trigger |
|-----------|------------------|------------------|
| Account Information | Until account deletion | User request or 2 years inactivity |
| Chat Messages | 90 days | Automatic purge |
| Extracted Memories | Duration of active account | Anonymized upon deletion |
| Payment Records | 7 years (legal requirement) | Per financial regulations |
| Usage Analytics | 26 months | Automatic anonymization |

## 5. Your Rights & Controls

### 5.1 GDPR & CCPA Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights:

| Right | Description | How to Exercise |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Access** | Request a copy of your data | Email privacy@BOOMO.app |
| **Rectification** | Correct inaccurate data | In-app Settings → Profile |
| **Erasure** | Delete your account and data | Settings → Account → Delete Account |
| **Portability** | Export your data | Settings → Privacy → Download Data |
| **Restriction** | Limit processing of your data | Contact privacy@BOOMO.app |
| **Objection** | Object to certain processing | Contact privacy@BOOMO.app |

### 5.2 In-App Privacy Controls

We provide granular privacy settings within the app:

- 🎭 **Anonymous Mode:** Option to use the app without linking to identifiable email
- 📊 **Analytics Opt-Out:** Disable usage analytics collection
- 🔔 **Memory Reset:** Clear AI character memories of past interactions
- 🗑️ **Auto-Delete:** Set automatic chat history deletion (daily/weekly/monthly)

### 5.3 Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time:
1. Go to **Settings → Account → Delete Account**
2. Confirm your decision (30-day grace period available)
3. Upon confirmation:
   - All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
   - Anonymized analytics data may be retained
   - Payment records retained as legally required

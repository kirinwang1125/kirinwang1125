# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 10, 2026**

## Introduction

Survival ("we," "our," or "the App") is an AI-driven post-apocalyptic survival text adventure game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our App. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Character Profile**: Your survivor name, backstory, age, gender, and core motivation you create within the game. This data is stored locally on your device.
- **Game Progress**: Story choices, inventory, timeline entries, and game state data stored locally on your device.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility purposes.
- **Usage Data**: Anonymous gameplay statistics such as days survived and actions taken, used to improve the game experience.
- **Purchase Data**: Transaction records processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system. We do not collect or store your payment information directly.

### AI-Generated Content
- **Story Content**: Your gameplay choices are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate personalized story narratives. These requests contain only your in-game character information and story context — no personal identifying information.
- **Image Generation**: Scene and character portrait descriptions are sent to AI image generation services. These contain only fictional game-related descriptions.

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the game experience
- To generate personalized AI-driven story content
- To save and restore your game progress
- To process in-app purchases
- To improve the App and fix bugs
- To provide customer support

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: All game progress data is stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage. Your data persists until you uninstall the App or manually reset your game progress.
- **Cloud Sync (Optional)**: Game data may be synced to our secure Supabase backend for backup purposes. This data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
- **AI Processing**: Story generation requests are processed by Google's Gemini API. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for information on how they handle API requests.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes only.

### Third-Party Services
- **Apple**: For In-App Purchase processing and App Store services
- **Google Gemini API**: For AI story and image generation
- **Supabase**: For optional cloud data backup

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your game data through the App
- **Delete** your data by resetting game progress in Settings or uninstalling the App
- **Opt-out** of cloud sync by playing offline

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:
- Local data encryption on device
- Secure HTTPS connections for all network requests
- API key protection and secure authentication

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
